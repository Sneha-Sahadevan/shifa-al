Spectra XT (Carbon Laser)

Also known as the "Hollywood Peel", this superior laser treatment combines a specialized carbon lotion to gently remove tired superficial skin layers, instantly revealing smoother, younger, and deeply radiant skin.

Key Benefits
  • Quick 20-minute sessions with absolutely zero downtime
  • Gentle dead skin removal for instant radiance and glow
Treatments
  • Unifies uneven skin tones and manages acne
  • Reduces large pores and pigmentation
  • Hair bleaching and fine hair removal
  • Collagen stimulation for mild skin tightening

Gentle Pro Max

An advanced laser technology specifically designed for permanent hair reduction. It features a proprietary Dynamic Cooling Device (DCD™) to protect skin and ensure maximum comfort.

Key Benefits
  • Versatile: Highly effective for all skin types (I-VI).
  • Enhanced Comfort: DCD cooling significantly reduces pain.
  • High Speed: 15 mins for small areas; under 90 mins for full body.
  • Safe: Minimal downtime and side effects.
Treatments
  • Permanent reduction of unwanted thick and fine hair
  • Professional hair removal for all body areas
  • Effective treatment regardless of skin pigmentation

ECO2 (Fractional Laser)

A highly dynamic fractional CO2 laser designed for intensive skin resurfacing. It offers remarkable results, often after a single treatment, with minimal downtime to restore your skin's youthful texture.

Key Benefits
  • Fast Sessions: A full face treatment typically takes 15–20 minutes.
  • Comfortable: Slight warm sensation with minimal discomfort (uses topical anesthetic).
  • Efficient: Rapid recovery with exceptionally minimal downtime.
  • Highly Effective: Delivers significant improvements often after just one session.
Treatments
  • Skin Resurfacing: Treatment of complex skin abnormalities.
  • Scars: Drastically fades acne, burn, and surgical scars.
  • Anti-Aging: Tightens skin laxity, fading deep wrinkles (face/neck/jaw).
  • Pore Refinement & Stretch Marks: Minimizes large pores and stretch marks.

Hydrafacial Device

An advanced, multi-functional medical-grade system offering comprehensive skin rejuvenation. It thoroughly cleanses, extracts impurities, and deeply hydrates using vital serums to deliver a flawless, deeply nourished complexion instantly.

Key Benefits
  • Deep Cleansing & Exfoliation: Gently removes dead skin, blackheads, and detoxifies.
  • Intense Hydration: Nourishes dry skin while controlling excess oil and sebum.
  • Anti-Aging: Reduces fine lines, early signs of aging, and boosts overall elasticity.
  • Universal Safety: Completely safe for all skin types with absolutely zero downtime.
Treatments
  • Routine acne reduction and breakout prevention
  • Advanced hydration therapy for dull and distressed skin
  • Complexion brightening and significant pore minimization
  • Preventative and restorative anti-aging facial procedures

Scalp Cleaning Device

Specialized clinical equipment structurally designed to deeply purify and revitalize your scalp. By gently exfoliating buildup and unclogging hair follicles, it creates the optimal environment for robust hair growth and vitality.

Key Benefits
  • Deep Cleanse & Oil Control: Removes dirt and strictly controls greasy scalp sebum.
  • Dandruff Relief: Gently exfoliates to powerfully eliminate dandruff and soothe irritation.
  • Follicle Unclogging: Drastically clears blocked follicles to promote thicker hair growth.
  • Nourishing: Massages and hydrates scalp to improve vital blood circulation.
Treatments
  • Premium preparation to enhance absorption for PRP and mesotherapy
  • Routine management and reversal of excessively oily or flaky scalps
  • Targeted therapy preventing hair fall directly caused by clogged pores
  • Comprehensive and safe maintenance for all scalp types with zero downtime
